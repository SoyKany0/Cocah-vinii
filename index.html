About me

Coach Vinicius Nunes is a former Brazilian competitive swimmer with over 15 years of experience in professional swimming coaching. His journey as an athlete gives him a deep understanding of the physical and mental demands of the sport, allowing him to coach with both precision and empathy.

He offers structured coaching classes for swimmers of all levels, focusing on stroke technique, efficiency, speed, and confidence in the water.

Focuses on efficient front-crawl technique, breathing control, and endurance for speed and distance.

Breaststroke

Develops proper timing, glide, and technique for smooth and efficient breaststroke swimming.
